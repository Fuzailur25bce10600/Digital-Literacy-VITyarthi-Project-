Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or passwords with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown apps
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Do not install screen-sharing apps when asked by unknown persons
- Regularly check your bank statements for unusual transactions

UPI / Financial Safety:
- Remember: money is received without entering a PIN; PIN is only needed to send money
- Double-check UPI IDs and names before making any payment

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
